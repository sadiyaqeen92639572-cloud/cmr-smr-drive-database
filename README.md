## Why CMR vs SMR matters

SMR drives collapse to 5–20 MB/s sustained write speed under NAS RAID rebuild workloads. A 4TB RAID rebuild that takes 6 hours on CMR can take 10+ days on SMR — causing controller timeouts and array failure.

**Always verify CMR before buying for NAS.**
